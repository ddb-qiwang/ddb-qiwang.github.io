Research Interests

    My research interest lies in the broad fields of applied statistics, machine learning and bioinformatics.
    I mainly work on developing annotation and clustering analyzing tools for single-cell RNA sequencing data, single-cell multi-omics data or single-cell resolution spatial transcriptome data.
